How to Install Stream Player on Android TV

Complete guide to install the IPTV player on Android TVs.

Step 1 – Enable Unknown Sources

To install external apps on Android devices, you must first enable installation from unknown sources.

  1. Open Settings on your Android TV
  2. Go to Security & Restrictions
  3. Select Unknown Sources
  4. Enable the option for the file manager or browser you will use

Step 2 – Install an App to Download APKs

You will need an app that allows downloading APK files from the internet.

One of the most used apps is Downloader.

  1. Open Google Play Store on your Android TV
  2. Search for Downloader
  3. Install the app

Step 3 – Download Stream Player

Once the Downloader app is installed:

  1. Open the Downloader app
  2. Enter the following URL:

https://tudominio.com/app/stream-player.apk

Then press Go to start the download.

Step 4 – Install the App

Once the APK file is downloaded:

  1. The Android installer will appear
  2. Press Install
  3. Wait for the installation to finish

After that, you can open the app from your Android TV apps menu.

Step 5 – Configure Stream Player

When you open the app for the first time, you can enter your IPTV data.

Stream Player is compatible with:

  • M3U Playlists
  • Xtream Codes Login
  • Movie and series libraries

Compatible Devices

Stream Player works on most Android TV devices:

  • Smart TVs with Android TV
  • Android TV Boxes
  • Devices with built-in Android TV
  • Amazon Fire TV Stick type devices compatible with manual installation
